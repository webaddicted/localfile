================================================================================
ABOUT THIS APP
================================================================================

Flutter Hive is an educational UI design demo app. Users browse sample screens
(login, buttons, text fields, lists, and more) to learn Flutter UI patterns.
No account registration is required to use the app.

================================================================================
WHAT DATA MAY BE DELETED
================================================================================

Because the app does not require an account, data is mainly collected
automatically by third-party services integrated for analytics, ads, crash
reporting, push notifications, and app configuration:

  • Device or other IDs (e.g. advertising ID, Firebase installation ID)
  • App interactions and screen views (Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics, Firebase Performance)
  • Push notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Remote configuration data (Firebase Remote Config)
  • Advertising-related data (Google AdMob)

When you submit a deletion request, we will work with Google/Firebase and
related providers to delete or anonymize data linked to your device where
technically possible.

================================================================================
WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT OR STORE ON OUR SERVERS
================================================================================

  • We do not require users to create an account
  • We do not store your name, email, or phone number on our own servers
  • Contact and SMS demo screens display sample data only — the app does not
    upload your real contacts or SMS messages to our servers
  • Camera, gallery, location, and similar features are used only for demo
    screens when you grant permission; that data is not uploaded by us

================================================================================
DATA THAT MAY BE KEPT
================================================================================

  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics that cannot identify you
  • Data we are required to retain for legal, security, or fraud-prevention
    purposes
  • Information held by third-party providers according to their own retention
    policies until deletion is completed

================================================================================
RETENTION PERIOD
================================================================================

  • Deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt
  • Backup or cached copies on third-party systems may take up to 90 additional
    days to be fully removed

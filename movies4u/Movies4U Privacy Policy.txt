When you use Movies4U, we and our third-party partners may automatically collect information such as:

- Ad interaction data (for example, impressions and clicks)

Some of this information may be considered personal data under applicable privacy laws.

The app may store preferences and settings locally on your device (for example, using SharedPreferences). This data stays on your device unless sent to a third-party service as described in this policy.

If you are located in the **European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland**, we request your consent before using certain data for personalized advertising, in line with applicable laws (including GDPR and the EU User Consent Policy).

When required, the app shows a consent message (via Google's User Messaging Platform) where you can:

Your consent choices are stored on your device and respected for future ad requests until you change them or they expire under applicable rules.
